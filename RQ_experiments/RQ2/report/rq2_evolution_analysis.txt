=== RQ2 INCREMENTAL COHORT-GROWTH VALIDATION ===

0. SETUP

   Base cohort: 22 models (the RQ1 set).
   Newcomers admitted one at a time, in ascending model id: M23, M24, M25, M26, M27, M28, M29, M30, M31, M32, M33
   Each cohort is renormalised on its own extremes; OTER refits its curve
   at every step and CIRC is recomputed in closed form.

1. ORDERING STABILITY OF THE ORIGINAL 22 MODELS

   tau/agreement are computed on the 22 original models only. The _base
   columns compare a step with the starting cohort (total drift); the _prev
   columns compare it with the step before (disruption of one admission).
   Agreement is the fraction of the 22 models holding the same rating.

2. HOW MUCH DOES AN INDIVIDUAL RATING MOVE?

   Variance of each original model's 12 ratings (t=0 through t=11). A model
   rated the same at every step contributes zero, so the maximum bounds how
   unsettled any single model ever was.

   LiveCodeBench OTER: mean variance 0.038, max 0.417 (M7); largest spread 2 class(es); 3 of 22 models have any spread at all.
      per-model variance: M7 0.417; M5 0.222; M12 0.188
   LiveCodeBench CIRC: every rating constant (variance = 0.000 for all 22 models).
   CodeXGLUE OTER: mean variance 0.032, max 0.188 (M14); largest spread 1 class(es); 7 of 22 models have any spread at all.
      per-model variance: M14 0.188; M3 0.139; M4 0.076; M9 0.076; M10 0.076; M17 0.076; M18 0.076
   CodeXGLUE CIRC: every rating constant (variance = 0.000 for all 22 models).

4. MODELS EXCLUDED FROM THE CURVE FIT

   changes when the excluded set changes and not merely when a model is
